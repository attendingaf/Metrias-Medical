The Execution Gap

Hospital case managers are drowning. The average SNF referral acceptance rate is 57%, meaning your team sends nearly seven referrals to place one patient. Prior authorization delays add 2+ days to rehab transfers. The dashboards flag these problems. Nobody has bandwidth to fix them.

Existing solutions predict delays. Metrias resolves them. Our expeditors make the follow up calls, chase the authorizations, and grind through rejections until the patient moves. We capture every operational barrier and intervention in structured data, building the attribution layer that proves what worked.

For Self-Insured Employers

Every extra hospital day costs you $2,500–$4,000. We get your people home faster.

When your employees are hospitalized, you pay per diem. Discharge delays aren't just a patient experience problem; they're a direct hit to your benefits spend. Metrias acts as a concierge discharge service for your covered lives, embedded in hospital workflows to accelerate safe transitions home.

Unlike telephonic navigators who call from outside the system, we're inside the discharge workflow, coordinating with hospitalists, case managers, and post-acute facilities in real time. We're not asking hospitals to pay for efficiency. We're proving savings to the payers who bear the cost.

Currently in structured pilot design with mid-size health systems. ROI models built on peer-reviewed LOS data. Targeting 0.5+ day reduction per intervention, translating to $1,250+ savings per case at conservative estimates.

For Capacity-Constrained Systems

If your ED is boarding admits, every blocked bed costs $8,000 to $15,000 in lost contribution margin, depending on service line.*

*(Range reflects medical vs. surgical service lines; KFF 2024)

At 85%+ occupancy, discharge delays aren't about saving money. They're about survival. Patients boarding in the ED cost nearly double what they'd cost on the floor. Surgical cases get canceled. Ambulances divert.

Metrias provides dedicated expeditor bandwidth to your case management team. We take the non-clinical logistics off their plates: SNF placement calls, transport coordination, DME orders, and prior auth follow-up, so your licensed staff can focus on complex clinical transitions.

Designed for 200+ bed systems at capacity. Demo mock coordination workflows validated. Operates alongside your EHR via secure virtual rounding. Integration available for high-volume deployments. US-based expeditors trained in hospital discharge workflows.
